Garden Planning Add-on

The Garden Planning Add-on for Blender enables users to simulate the growth of various plants through computational models. Using Python scripting in Blender, this add-on creates dynamic plant structures, simulating growth patterns for plants like sunflowers and baby's breath. The add-on leverages L-systems and randomization to generate plant branching, offering a glimpse into nature's beauty through code.

ZuhairaTech
"Blending computational algorithms with nature's intricate design."

In this project, L-systems were adapted to generate plant growth models in Blender using Python scripting. Growth patterns such as branching angles, plant height, and radius were controlled based on predefined rules for each plant type.


                    # Example code for setting plant properties
                    plant_rules = {
                        "SUNFLOWER": {
                            "angle_of_branching": 0,
                            "radius": 0.1,
                            "height": 0.5
                        },
                        "BABYSBREATH": {
                            "angle_of_branching": 1,
                            "radius": 0.01,
                            "height": 0.2
                        }
                    }

While the current implementation of L-systems for modeling plant growth demonstrates the potential for computational simulations, there are several areas for future exploration and improvement:

  • Refining Plant Models: Future work could focus on refining the models of plants, especially sunflowers and baby's breath, by incorporating more accurate growth rules based on real-world data.
  • Implementing Specific Plant Rules: Implementing detailed and specific growth rules for a wider range of plants, considering environmental factors such as light, soil, and temperature.
  • Expanding the Scope of L-systems: Exploring the application of L-systems in simulating other natural phenomena like tree growth, coral reefs, or even architectural structures.
  • Improving Randomness in Growth Patterns: Enhancing the randomness in the branching patterns of plants to more closely resemble real-life variability in plant growth.
  • 3D Visualization and Integration: Developing a 3D visualization of plant growth to provide a more interactive and immersive experience for users.
  • Optimization and Performance: Optimizing the code to handle more complex plant structures, allowing for larger-scale simulations and real-time rendering.
